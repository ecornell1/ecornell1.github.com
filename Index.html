Elizabeth Cornell

Elizabeth Cornell is a Pre-Doctoral fellow in English at Fordham University. She is the Project Coordinator for the Keywords Collaboratory, a member of the Fordham University Digital Humanities Working Group, founder of Fordham's Graduate Student Digital Humanities group, and an active participant in the larger digital humanities community in New York City. In addition, she is a contributor to Digital Yoknapatawpha, a map-based online archive of William Faulkner's work, published by the Digital Media Lab at the University of Virginia.
